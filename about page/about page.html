About our Business

Sou and Mike's Cookies have been in business for about 2 years. They built this business after graduating from Fresno State. They both majored in Information Systems and that helped build their business they have today.

The store is located in the Riverpark area by Edwards Theater. Their cookies are made fresh and in-house. There are ten employees that run the day-to-day operation. The store has made it into the Fresno Bee as the "Best Cookies in Town." Yelp users rate them 5 stars.

We look forward to seeing everyone in town. Come try out our delicious cookies. Come in and say how you know Sou or Mike and get a 10% discount on your purchase. We will have new cookies for the upcoming season.
